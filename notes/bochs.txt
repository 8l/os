Configuration required for Bochs:

Required:
--enable-x86-64 --enable-acpi  --enable-pci

Recommended:
* debugger
* "x86 debugger" (not sure what the difference is)

--enable-debugger --enable-x86-debugger

Optimization flags:
--enable-all-optimizations
CFLAGS='-fomit-frame-pointer -O2 -march=native'
CXXFLAGS='-fomit-frame-pointer -O2 -march=native'
--enable-trace-cache --enable-repeat-speedups


Whole shebang:
'./configure'  '-C' '--prefix=/opt/bochs' '--enable-debugger' '--enable-x86-64' '--enable-all-optimizations' --enable-acpi CFLAGS='-fomit-frame-pointer -O2 -march=native'  CXXFLAGS='-fomit-frame-pointer -O2 -march=native' --enable-trace-cache --enable-repeat-speedups --enable-x86-debugger --enable-pci
